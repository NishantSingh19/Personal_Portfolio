About -

Hi, I'm Nishant Kumar Singh 👋, a passionate developer and lifelong learner and I've completed my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at the School of Management Sciences, Lucknow 🎓. My journey into tech started with a curiosity for how websites and applications work, which soon grew into a passion for building projects that combine creativity with problem-solving.

I work mainly with Python 🐍, JavaScript ⚡, Flask, and SQL 🗄️, and I enjoy bringing ideas to life by creating responsive and practical web applications. Along the way, I've also developed a strong interest in data science 📊 and artificial intelligence 🤖. Exploring datasets, visualizing patterns, and applying machine learning concepts excites me because it bridges the gap between data and real-world decision-making.

Through certifications like Data Science with Python (CodeWithHarry) ✅, Introduction to Generative AI (Google Cloud ☁️), and the HackerRank Python Certificate 🏆, I've built a strong foundation in both web development and data-driven problem-solving. These credentials are not just milestones, but proof of my dedication to learning new skills and applying them effectively.

I've worked on many projects that reflect my interests. Some of my favorites include a YouTube Manager 🎥 web application built with Flask and SQLite, a Weather App ⛅ that fetches live data using APIs, and a Train Reservation System 🚆 that shows real-world ticket booking. I also created a Jarvis Assistant 🤖 that automates simple tasks. Each project has helped me improve not just my technical skills, but also my ability to think critically, debug effectively, and design user-friendly solutions.

During my internship at CodSoft 💼, I worked on frontend projects like a personal portfolio website 🌐, which taught me a lot about responsive design, structuring code, and solving real development challenges. This hands-on experience helped me grow into a more confident developer who understands the importance of both functionality and design.

Looking ahead 🚀, my goal is to build a career where I can apply my skills in real-world projects 🌍 that have a meaningful impact. I want to contribute to solutions that not only solve technical problems but also improve lives. With a mix of web development, data science, and AI skills, I'm preparing myself to take on challenges that push me out of my comfort zone and help me grow as a versatile developer.
